Yuntao Du (杜云滔)

Data Privacy & Trustworthy Machine Learning

I am a PhD student in computer science at Purdue University, advised by Prof. Ninghui Li. My research interests lie in Data Privacy and Privacy in Machine Learning. I am also broadly interested in analyzing emerging privacy risks arising from recent advances in AI. Here is my CV.

My research has been recognized and supported by the Ross Fellowship (2023-2027), Presidential Doctoral Excellence Awards (2023-2027), and Herbold Scholarship (2023-2024).
